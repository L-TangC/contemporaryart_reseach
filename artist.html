袁廣鳴
Goang-Ming Yuan

Goang-Ming Yuan, the most famous video artist from Taiwan. He started his career in 1984, using metaphor and technology to symbolize the life of every human being. Also, the artwork has a very poetic and in-depth display of human perception and consciousness. Goang-Ming includes Iconology, Social History, and postcolonialism theory in his career. It not only describes our life but also shows Taiwan's history and Global issues.
